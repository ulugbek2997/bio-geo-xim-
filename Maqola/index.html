It was commented that dictatorships about coronavirus are being administered to children in Uzbek schools

The Ministry notes that the Ministry of Health is taking measures to ensure the sanitary-epidemiological well-being of the
population and prevent the spread and spread of the disease in the country.

Recommendations have been developed to protect the population against coronavirus. In order to convey these recommendations in a simple language to the younger generation, dictatorship writing activities have been established in schools. “During the dictation writing, students will learn about the ways in which coronavirus can be transmitted and what measures should be taken to prevent its spread.
